# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated**: January 2025

## 1. Introduction

Welcome to **The Fox Within** ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about how we collect, use, and protect your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding data collection, use, and disclosure when you use our mobile application ("App").

By using our App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Account Information
- **Email Address**: Used for account registration, authentication, and communication
- **Username**: Optional display name for your account
- **Password**: Encrypted and securely stored (we cannot see your password)

### 2.2 Application Activity Data
We collect the following data to provide core app functionality:

- **Daily Tasks**: Tasks you create and complete each day
- **Task History**: Records of completed tasks and activities
- **Daily Reviews**: Your daily reflections, achievements, and satisfaction scores
- **Tomorrow Plans**: Plans and goals you set for upcoming days
- **Focus Sessions**: Records of your focus and productivity sessions
- **Learning Progress**: Progress through structured courses and learning materials
- **Course Notes**: Notes you take while completing courses
- **Random Thoughts**: Personal thoughts and ideas you record
- **Rest Activities**: Records of rest and relaxation activities
- **Onboarding Answers**: Responses to initial setup questions (used for personalization)

### 2.3 Device Information
- **Device Type**: Basic device information for compatibility purposes
- **Operating System**: OS version for app optimization
- **App Version**: For troubleshooting and support

### 2.4 Notification Preferences
- **Notification Settings**: Your preferences for receiving notifications
- **Notification Times**: Scheduled times for reminders and alerts

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

### 3.1 Core App Functionality
- **Account Management**: Creating and managing your user account
- **Data Synchronization**: Syncing your data across devices
- **Task Management**: Storing and managing your tasks, notes, and records
- **Personalization**: Customizing your app experience based on your preferences

### 3.2 Service Improvement
- **App Optimization**: Improving app performance and user experience
- **Bug Fixes**: Identifying and resolving technical issues
- **Feature Development**: Understanding how features are used to guide future development

### 3.3 Communication
- **Support**: Responding to your inquiries and providing customer support
- **Important Updates**: Notifying you about important app updates or changes

**We do NOT use your data for:**
- ❌ Advertising or marketing to third parties
- ❌ Selling your personal information
- ❌ Sharing your data with third parties for their own purposes

## 4. Data Storage and Location

### 4.1 Local Storage
Your data is stored locally on your device using:
- **SQLite Database**: Encrypted local database for app data
- **Secure Storage**: Encrypted storage for sensitive information (using FlutterSecureStorage)

### 4.2 Cloud Storage
We use **Supabase** as our cloud storage provider to enable data synchronization across your devices. 

**Data Location**: Your data is stored on Supabase servers located in **North America** (United States). This ensures compliance with data residency requirements for users in the United States and Canada.

**Data Security**: All data transmitted to and stored in Supabase is encrypted using industry-standard encryption protocols (HTTPS/TLS).

## 5. Data Sharing and Third-Party Services

### 5.1 Supabase
We share your data with **Supabase** solely for the purpose of:
- Data storage and backup
- Data synchronization across devices
- User authentication

Supabase is a trusted service provider that adheres to strict data security standards and privacy practices. Supabase does not use your data for their own purposes.

### 5.2 No Other Third-Party Sharing
We do **NOT**:
- Sell your personal information to third parties
- Share your data with advertisers
- Allow third parties to access your data for their own purposes

## 6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

- **Encryption in Transit**: All data transmitted between your device and our servers uses HTTPS/TLS encryption
- **Encryption at Rest**: Sensitive data is encrypted when stored locally and in the cloud
- **Secure Authentication**: User authentication is handled securely through Supabase Auth
- **Access Controls**: Data is isolated per user, and users can only access their own data
- **Regular Security Updates**: We regularly update our app and infrastructure to address security vulnerabilities

## 7. Your Rights and Choices

### 7.1 Access Your Data
You can access all your data directly through the App. You can view:
- Your tasks and task history
- Your daily reviews and notes
- Your learning progress
- All other data stored in your account

### 7.2 Delete Your Data
You have the right to delete your data at any time:

**Through the App**:
- Use the "Data Management" feature in Settings to delete specific data or your entire account

**By Contacting Us**:
- Email us at **thefoxwithinapp@gmail.com** to request data deletion
- We will process your request within 30 days

### 7.3 Export Your Data
You can export your data at any time:
- Use the "Export Data" feature in Settings to download a copy of all your data in JSON format

### 7.4 Withdraw Consent
You can withdraw your consent to data processing at any time by:
- Deleting your account through the App
- Contacting us at **thefoxwithinapp@gmail.com**

### 7.5 Opt-Out of Notifications
You can manage notification preferences in Settings:
- Enable or disable specific notification types
- Adjust notification times
- Disable all notifications if desired

## 8. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide our services. If you delete your account:

- **Immediate Deletion**: Your account and associated data will be deleted from our systems
- **Backup Data**: Any backup data will be deleted within 30 days
- **Local Data**: You can delete local data through the App's data management features

## 10. California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) Rights

If you are a California resident, you have the following additional rights:

### 10.1 Right to Know
You have the right to know:
- What personal information we collect
- How we use your personal information
- Whether we sell or share your personal information (we do NOT)

### 10.2 Right to Delete
You have the right to request deletion of your personal information, subject to certain exceptions.

### 10.3 Right to Opt-Out
You have the right to opt-out of the sale of personal information (we do not sell personal information).

### 10.4 Right to Non-Discrimination
We will not discriminate against you for exercising your CCPA rights.

**To exercise your CCPA rights**, please contact us at **thefoxwithinapp@gmail.com** with the subject line "CCPA Request."

## 11. Canadian Privacy Rights (PIPEDA)

If you are a Canadian resident, you have the following rights under the Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA):

### 11.1 Right to Access
You have the right to access your personal information and know how it is being used.

### 11.2 Right to Correction
You have the right to request correction of inaccurate or incomplete personal information.

### 11.3 Right to Withdraw Consent
You can withdraw your consent to the collection, use, or disclosure of your personal information at any time.

### 11.4 Right to File a Complaint
If you have concerns about how we handle your personal information, you can file a complaint with the Office of the Privacy Commissioner of Canada.

**To exercise your PIPEDA rights**, please contact us at **thefoxwithinapp@gmail.com** with the subject line "PIPEDA Request."

**Note**: Your data is stored on servers located in North America (United States). While this may involve cross-border data transfer, we ensure that appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your information.
